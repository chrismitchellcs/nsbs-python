Service
Our service department is tidy, fast, and staffed with competent mechanics who are eager to see you back on your bike. Our turn-around time is unmatched anywhere else in town. We fix everything bicycle related.
At NSBS we operate on a first come first serve basis. Bring your bike in anytime and leave it with us for some time depending on how busy it is. We will give an accurate estimate of how long you will be with out it. Feel free to call or email to get the current ETA for service. You do not need to call ahead to book your bike in.

While our store focuses on mountain bikes, we do have parts and can repair all types of bikes. We are happy to fix any problem, just bring your bike and we will get you riding soon!
